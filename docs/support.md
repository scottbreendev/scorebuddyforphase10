## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**Q: How do I add players?**  
Go to the **Players** tab and tap the **+** button. You can create a new player by typing a name, or import from Contacts to get their profile picture. No permissions are required to add players manually.

**Q: How do I start a game?**  
Tap **Start New Game** from the Home screen (or the **+** in the Games tab). Select 2–6 players, arrange the dealer order by dragging players into position, then tap **Start Game**.

**Q: What's the dealer order?**  
The first player in the list deals the first round. After each round, the deal passes clockwise (down the list). You can change the order at any time during a game via the **⋯ menu → Dealer Order**.

---

### Scoring & Gameplay

**Q: How do I record a round?**  
In an active game, tap the orange **Record Round** button. Enter each player's score and toggle the phase switch if they completed their phase this round.

**Q: What happens when a player completes Phase 10?**  
The game ends automatically. Score Buddy calculates the final standings — the player who completed Phase 10 wins (lowest score among tied phase completers).

**Q: How do I record a Skip card?**  
Swipe right on the player who was skipped. The Skip Entry sheet opens with them pre-selected as the target. Tap who played the Skip card, and it saves automatically.

Alternatively, tap **⋯ → Quick Actions → Record Skip** to open the skip sheet manually.

**Q: Can I undo a Skip card?**  
Yes — for 30 seconds after recording a Skip, an Undo banner appears at the top of the active game screen. Tap **Undo** to remove it. After 30 seconds, it can't be undone automatically, but you can delete it from the **Skip History** sheet.

**Q: I made a mistake in the last round. Can I fix it?**  
Yes. In an active game, tap **⋯ → Edit Last Round**. This opens the round editor pre-populated with the saved values. Correct the scores or phases and tap **Save**. Only the most recently recorded round can be edited this way.

**Q: How do I delete a player from an active game?**  
Swipe left on the player's row in the active game and tap **Remove**. A confirmation prompt will appear.

### Statistics & Insights

**Q: Why are my stats showing "Last 3 games"?**  
Free users see statistics computed over their last 3 games. Upgrade to Pro to see stats across your full history with no cap.

**Q: What does "Stats from completed games only" mean?**  
This setting (on by default in **Settings**) means best/worst score stats only count games where someone actually completed Phase 10. This prevents abandoned or paused games from skewing your averages. You can turn it off in Settings if you prefer to include all games.

**Q: What are Player Titles?**  
Player Titles are dynamic labels computed from your stats — they're never stored and update as you play more games. Examples: **The Aggressor** (plays lots of Skip cards), **Comeback Kid** (often wins after trailing), **Home Court Hero** (wins much more at a specific location). Pro feature.

**Q: How is "Most Stuck Phase" calculated?**  
It's based on average consecutive rounds you spent on a phase without completing it — not just how many times you failed it. This makes it a better measure of which phase actually blocks progress.

### Location Features

**Q: Do I have to share my location?**  
No. Location is completely optional and requires you to explicitly grant permission. If you don't grant permission, the app works fully without it.

**Q: What does Score Buddy do with my location?**  
If you tag a game with a location, Score Buddy uses your approximate location (reduced accuracy for privacy) to generate a human-readable name (e.g. "Melbourne, Australia"). This is stored locally on your device only. It's used to show you stats like "you win more games at home". Your location is never sent to any server other than Apple's reverse geocoding service.

**Q: Can I add a location manually without granting permission?**  
Not currently — the location name is generated from your GPS coordinates. If you deny permission, you won't be able to tag games with a location.

### Privacy & Data

**Q: Where is my data stored?**  
All your game data (players, scores, round history) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. It is included in your iCloud Backup if you have that enabled in iOS Settings.

**Q: Does Score Buddy collect my player names or scores?**  
No. Player names, scores, and game content never leave your device.

**Q: What analytics does Score Buddy collect?**  
Score Buddy uses Firebase Analytics (without advertising identifiers) to collect anonymous, aggregated usage data — things like "a game was started" or "the paywall was viewed". No player names, scores, or personal information are ever included. See the [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.html) for full details.

**Q: How do I delete all my data?**  
Go to **Settings → Data & Storage → Delete All Data**. This permanently removes all players, games, and stats from your device. Alternatively, deleting the app removes all local data.

### Purchases & Billing

**Q: Is Score Buddy Pro a subscription?**  
No. Score Buddy Pro is a **one-time purchase** — pay once, use forever. There are no recurring charges.

**Q: What's included in the free version?**  
See the Free vs Pro table above. The free version is fully functional for tracking games — you're capped at 3 saved games and stats from your last 3 games.

**Q: I bought Pro but it's not showing as active.**  
Try restoring your purchase: **Settings → Score Buddy Pro → Restore Purchases**. If that doesn't work, check that you're signed in to the same Apple ID you purchased with. If the problem persists, [contact support](mailto:scott.breen.dev@outlook.com?subject=Score%20Buddy%20Pro%20Purchase%20Issue).

**Q: Can I use Pro on multiple devices?**  
Yes. Score Buddy Pro is tied to your Apple ID. Sign in with the same Apple ID on another device and restore purchases to unlock Pro.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**  
All purchases are processed by Apple. For refunds, visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com).

### Live Activities

**Q: Why don't I see Live Activities on my iPad?**  
Live Activities (Dynamic Island / Lock Screen updates) are an iPhone-only feature — this is an Apple platform limitation that applies to all apps, not just Score Buddy.

**Q: Live Activities aren't showing up on my iPhone.**  
Check that Live Activities are enabled for Score Buddy: **iOS Settings → Score Buddy → Live Activities → Allow**. Also verify you have a Pro subscription, as Live Activities are a Pro feature.

### Technical

**Q: What iOS version does Score Buddy require?**  
Score Buddy requires **iOS 26.0 or later**.

**Q: Does Score Buddy support iPad?**  
Yes. Score Buddy is optimised for both iPhone and iPad, including a two-column layout for larger screens. Note: Live Activities are iPhone-only (Apple limitation).

**Q: The app crashed. How do I report it?**  
Please [email support](mailto:scott.breen.dev@outlook.com?subject=Score%20Buddy%20Crash%20Report) with:
- What you were doing when it crashed
- Your iPhone/iPad model
- Your iOS version
- Approximate time of the crash

Score Buddy uses Firebase Crashlytics to automatically capture crash reports (anonymously), which helps us fix issues quickly.
